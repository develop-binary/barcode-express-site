About Barcode Express

Transforming manufacturing inventory management for modern production

Our Mission

At Barcode Express, we're dedicated to revolutionizing manufacturing operations through intelligent inventory management and production tracking. Our mission is to provide manufacturers with powerful tools that streamline assembly line operations, predict demand accurately, and maximize production efficiency.

What We Do

Barcode Express is a comprehensive manufacturing inventory management platform that adapts to any production environment. We combine multi-stage assembly line tracking with advanced sales velocity analysis to help manufacturers optimize their entire production process. Our platform integrates seamlessly with major e-commerce platforms including Amazon, Shopify, eBay, Gunbroker, Etsy, and Wish, providing real-time visibility across your entire operation.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike generic inventory systems, Barcode Express understands the unique challenges of multi-stage manufacturing. We provide specialized tools for sequential assembly line tracking, workforce capacity analysis, and bottleneck detection. Our predictive analytics don't just track what's happened—they forecast manufacturing velocity based on sales trends, enabling you to stay ahead of demand and avoid costly stockouts or overproduction.

Our Technology

Our platform combines advanced barcode tracking with sophisticated analytics and machine learning algorithms. We track products through every stage of the assembly line, analyze sales patterns across multiple platforms, and automatically generate work orders based on predictive demand modeling. This integrated approach gives you complete control over your manufacturing operations and inventory management.

We're a passionate team of manufacturing specialists, software engineers, and data scientists committed to delivering the most comprehensive inventory management solution for modern manufacturers. Our deep understanding of production workflows and e-commerce operations enables us to build tools that truly address the challenges manufacturers face every day.
